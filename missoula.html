Meet the founder

After considering traditional and alternative Kindergarten options for my son, I realized there was an opportunity to create a microschool where kids learn core academics at their own level and pace, and life skills that build real-world confidence and independence.

So I invited experienced educators (including Esther Wojcicki, educator and author of How to Raise Successful People), a community of families, and seasoned edtech entrepreneurs — the content providers behind AI tutors used by millions of students — to co-build the school we've all been dreaming of.

The first one opens in Missoula, where my kids are growing up as second generation Montanans. We'd love for your family to be part of it.

The neoschool model

Is the neoschool model proven?
Yes. The 2-hour academics model is used by microschools nationally with strong outcomes on third-party assessments. The AI tutoring component is backed by a 2025 Harvard RCT (Kestin et al., Scientific Reports) showing AI tutoring produces 2× learning gains vs. best-practice classroom instruction. Our project-based learning approach is supported by Lucas Education Research (2021–2022). We collect our own data from day one and share results openly with our community.
Will kids be sitting on screens for 2 hours straight?
Not at all. The 2-hour academic block at neoschool Missoula is a series of short, mastery-based cycles, typically 10–15 minutes per subject, with brain breaks and movement built in between. Students rotate between instructor-led small groups, independent AI-adaptive practice, and hands-on activities. Screens are one tool among many.
How long is the neoschool day?
The neoschool day runs 8:30am–4:30pm, fully included in tuition, so no separate childcare is needed. The afternoon is filled with real projects, life skills workshops, outdoor play, and sports. neoschool Missoula is designed to work for working families.
What is the neoschool attendance policy?
Flexible. Family vacations and life happen, and we don't penalize families for them. Students can stay on track during time away through the neoschool digital platform from anywhere.

Academics

Do students at neoschool Missoula still learn reading, math, and science?
Absolutely. Reading, writing, math, and science are the foundation of the 2-hour daily academic block. What's different is how: AI tutors adapt to each child's exact level and pace, so a student who is ahead in math and behind in reading gets a different experience than their classmate. Every child gets a personalized curriculum, not a one-size-fits-all classroom.
Do students really learn more in only 2 hours a day?
The research says yes. A 2025 Harvard RCT found AI tutoring produces 2× learning gains compared to best-practice classroom instruction. Traditional school days are long, but most of that time is lost to transitions, classroom management, and waiting. neoschool focuses on high-quality, high-engagement learning in short, mastery-based cycles.
Is neoschool Missoula an online school?
No. neoschool is a physical campus in Missoula, Montana with on-site educators, real classrooms, outdoor space, and a full school day. It is not a virtual school or homeschool program, though we do offer part-time drop-in enrollment for homeschooling families.

AI & Technology

Is the AI tutor replacing the teacher at neoschool?
No. For Kindergarten and 1st grade, neoschool is instructor-led first. Our educators are in the room every day, leading small-group instruction, providing real-time feedback, and building relationships with students. The AI tutor supplements — adapting practice to each child's level and pace — freeing educators to spend more time mentoring, connecting, and inspiring.
How does neoschool approach AI safety for young children?
Thoughtfully. We use age-appropriate, education-specific AI tools, not general-purpose chatbots. Screen time is intentional and time-limited. We follow COPPA guidelines, never use student data for advertising, and are transparent with families about every tool we use. Our goal is a healthy, empowered, age-appropriate relationship with technology.

Life skills

Why does neoschool Missoula focus on life skills?
Because the world our kids are growing up in demands more than academic knowledge. Financial literacy, emotion regulation, communication, entrepreneurship, cooking, gardening, and survival skills aren't extras — they're the foundation of a capable, confident, independent human. And Missoula is the perfect place to teach them.
What life skills workshops can families expect at neoschool?
Workshops rotate throughout the year and include cooking, gardening, survival skills, financial literacy, public speaking, entrepreneurship, emotion regulation, and more. We bring in community experts and local Missoula partners. Families always know what's coming and are welcome to suggest topics.

Application, enrollment & tuition

How much does it cost to apply to neoschool Missoula?
Nothing. Applying is completely free.
What does neoschool tuition cover?
Full tuition covers the complete school day (8:30am–4:30pm), all academic instruction, AI tutor access, life skills workshops, healthy snacks, sports, forest days, field trips, project and maker materials, and access to the neoschool digital platform. No hidden fees.
I'm a Missoula homeschooling family. Does neoschool offer part-time enrollment?
Yes. Half-day drop-in enrollment includes access to life skills workshops, outdoor play, and the neoschool community. It's designed for homeschooling families in Missoula who want their kids to benefit from the neoschool experience without switching from their current academic setup. Reach out to learn more about part-time enrollment options.
