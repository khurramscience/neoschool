Understanding Division

What is Division?

Division is splitting a large group of objects into smaller equal groups. The result is called the quotient (whole number part).

Example: 12 ÷ 3 = 4 (quotient)

Division with Remainder

Sometimes we can't divide objects equally. The leftover objects are called the remainder. We get both a quotient (whole groups) and a remainder (leftover).

Example: 14 ÷ 3 = 4 (quotient) remainder 2

Key Terms

Dividend: The number being divided
Divisor: The number we divide by
Quotient: The result of division
Remainder: What's left over
