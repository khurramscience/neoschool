# 🔢 Even and Odd Numbers Laboratory

An interactive web-based learning tool designed to help students understand and practice the concepts of even and odd numbers through engaging activities.

## 🌟 Features

### 📚 Theory Section
- Comprehensive explanation of even and odd numbers
- Visual examples and clear definitions
- Addition rules with visual representations
- Quick tips for easy memorization

### 🎮 Practice Game
- Interactive addition game
- Predict whether the sum will be even or odd
- Progressive difficulty levels
- Real-time scoring and feedback
- Visual color coding (blue for even, red for odd)

### ✅ Knowledge Check
- 10-question quiz to test understanding
- Multiple choice questions
- Immediate feedback with explanations
- Progress tracking
- Final score with grade assessment

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open the Application**
   - Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser
   - No additional setup or installation required

2. **Navigate Through Sections**
   - Use the navigation buttons at the top to switch between sections
   - Start with the Theory section to learn the concepts
   - Practice with the Game section
   - Test your knowledge with the Quiz section

3. **Theory Section**
   - Read through the explanations
   - Pay attention to the addition rules
   - Review the examples and tips

4. **Practice Game**
   - Click "Start New Game" to begin
   - Two numbers will appear - add them mentally
   - Predict if the result will be even or odd
   - Click the appropriate button (🔵 Even or 🔴 Odd)
   - Watch your score and level increase with correct answers

5. **Knowledge Check**
   - Answer 10 multiple-choice questions
   - Get immediate feedback on each answer
   - See your final score and grade at the end
   - Click "Try Again" to retake the quiz

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

Students will learn to:
- Identify even and odd numbers
- Understand the patterns in number endings
- Apply addition rules for even and odd numbers:
  - Even + Even = Even
  - Even + Odd = Odd
  - Odd + Odd = Even
- Develop mental math skills
- Build confidence in number recognition
