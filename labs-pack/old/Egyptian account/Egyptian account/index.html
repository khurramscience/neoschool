How to Read Egyptian Numbers

The Egyptians used an additive system — numbers were written as the sum of symbols:

3247 →

3 × 1000 + 2 × 100 + 4 × 10 + 7 × 1 = 3247

Writing Rules

  • Symbols are arranged from left to right in descending order
  • Identical symbols are grouped together
  • The number is read as the sum of all symbols
  • There was no zero in the Egyptian system!
