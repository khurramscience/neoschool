# Even and Odd Numbers - Interactive Math Laboratory

An interactive web-based mathematics laboratory designed to help students learn and practice identifying even and odd numbers.

## Features

### 📚 Theory Section
- **Comprehensive Explanation**: Clear definitions and rules for even and odd numbers
- **Visual Examples**: Color-coded number grids showing even (blue) and odd (red) numbers
- **Interactive Demo**: Click on numbers to see if they're even or odd with instant feedback
- **Learning Rules**: Simple rules to remember (numbers ending in 0,2,4,6,8 are even; 1,3,5,7,9 are odd)

### 🎯 Practice Section
- **Exercise 1**: Identify if a random number is even or odd
  - Real-time scoring
  - Immediate feedback with explanations
  - Progressive difficulty
  
- **Exercise 2**: Sort numbers into even and odd categories
  - Drag and drop functionality
  - Visual sorting areas
  - Score tracking

### 📝 Test Section
- **10 Question Test**: Comprehensive assessment of understanding
- **Timer**: 30 seconds per question to encourage quick thinking
- **Results Summary**: Detailed score breakdown with performance feedback
- **Retry Option**: Take the test multiple times to improve

## How to Use

1. **Open the Application**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Navigate**: Use the navigation buttons to switch between Theory, Practice, and Test sections
3. **Learn**: Start with the Theory section to understand the concepts
4. **Practice**: Use the Practice section to reinforce your learning
5. **Test**: Take the final test to assess your understanding
