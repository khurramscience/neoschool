Fractions: Mixed Numbers

Explore fractions while you help yourself to 1 and 1/2 pizzas and wash it down with 1/3 a cola! Create your own fractions using fun interactive objects. Match shapes and numbers.

How to use:
Select a fraction (e.g., 1/2, 1/3, 1/4), then add or remove pieces of pizza or cola. The visual and numeric value will update. Try to match the target values: 1 and 1/2 pizzas and 1/3 cola!
