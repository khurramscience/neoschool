How to Play:

  1. Choose your difficulty level
  2. Click on a mosaic piece to select it (it will glow blue)
  3. Click on an available puzzle to place the selected number
  4. Each correct answer earns points and reveals part of the mosaic
  5. Complete all puzzles to see your final score!

Tip: You can also drag and drop pieces if you prefer!
