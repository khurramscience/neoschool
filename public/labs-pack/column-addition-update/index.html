How Place Values Work in Addition

📊 Place Values

Each digit in a number has its place - the place value:

2 4 7
hundreds tens ones

➕ Column Addition

In column addition, we add numbers by place values, starting from the right:

Step 1: Add the ones

   1
  2 4 7
+ 1 3 5
─────
        2

7 + 5 = 12. Write 2 in ones place, carry 1 to tens place (shown above).

Step 2: Add the tens

   1
  2 4 7
+ 1 3 5
─────
     8 2

4 + 3 + 1 (carry) = 8. Write 8 in tens place.

Step 3: Add the hundreds

2 4 7
+ 1 3 5
─────
3 8 2

2 + 1 = 3. Write 3 in hundreds place.

Result: 247 + 135 = 382
