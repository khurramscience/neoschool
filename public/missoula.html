The neoschool experience

Personalized Academics

Two focused hours blending AI-adaptive learning with proven classroom best practices: peer instruction, small-group activities, and real-time instructor feedback. Brain breaks between subjects.

🔨

Real-World Projects

Hands-on projects that connect classroom learning to the world outside. Make, build, present, have fun.

🌳

Outdoor Play + Sports

Unstructured time outside and sports every day. Dedicated forest days. Movement and free play improve attention.

Meet the founder

After considering traditional and alternative Kindergarten options for my son, I realized there was an opportunity to create a microschool where kids learn core academics at their own level and pace, and life skills that build real-world confidence and independence.

So I invited experienced educators (including Esther Wojcicki, educator and author of How to Raise Successful People), a community of families, and seasoned edtech entrepreneurs — the content providers behind AI tutors used by millions of students — to co-build the school we've all been dreaming of.

The first one opens in Missoula, where my kids are growing up as second generation Montanans. We'd love for your family to be part of it.

— Jennie Yang
