📚 Theoretical Material

What is multiplication?

Multiplication is a mathematical operation that allows you to quickly add the same numbers.

Example: 3 × 4 = 3 + 3 + 3 + 3 = 12

Read as: "three times four equals twelve"

Components of multiplication

Sign ×
Multiplier 4
Equals sign =
Product 12

Properties of multiplication

  • Commutative property: a × b = b × a
  • Associative property: (a × b) × c = a × (b × c)
  • Distributive property: a × (b + c) = a × b + a × c
  • Multiplication by 1: a × 1 = a
  • Multiplication by 0: a × 0 = 0
